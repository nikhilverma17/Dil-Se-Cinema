Tu Jhoothi Mai Makkar

9/10

If you're a fan of romantic comedies, then you definitely don't want to miss out this movie. The movie revolves around the concept of breakup consultancy and how it brings two people closer. The movie's plot is well written and executed, keeping the audience engaged throughout. The concept of breakup consultancy is fresh and unique, and the way it is woven into the story is seamless. The movie also deals with the theme of family values and the importance of living together as a family. It beautifully portrays the Indian family system and how it still holds its values in modern times. The music of the movie is foot-tapping and goes well with the story. The locations used in the movie are picturesque and will make you want to pack your bags and head to Spain. Overall, it is a must-watch movie for all rom-com lovers. The chemistry between Ranbir Kapoor and Shraddha Kapoor is the highlight of the movie, and their performances are top-notch. The movie has a perfect balance of humor, emotions, and drama, making it a complete package. Anubhav Singh Bassi was amazing As a debut his comic time was outstanding he got less dialogue if he also got a more dialogue may be he could overtake Ranbir Kapoor. So, grab your popcorn and head to the nearest theatre to witness the magic.
